Design philosophy


1. This is designed specifically for Google's unmanned shared taxi brand Waymo car interface, with the development of science and technology, driverless cars will gradually more people use.


2. Through my research, I've found that car owners who don't share their cars will pay more attention to entertainment, and the car's on screen interface will gradually shift its focus to entertainment.


3. The use of more popular gradient sci-fi color, so that the screen more eye-catching, combined with the WAYMO brand more natural.
