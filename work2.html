Processing robot design


Name: robot

Birth: 2019-5-25

Tool: AI PS Processing

Type: Interfacedesign

Propartles: persion









Interaction description


1. The robot ascends at a uniform speed. When the w key is pressed, it accelerates to rise, and when the s key is pressed, it falls

2. When the mouse clicks, the robot's expression will change, from calm to joy.

3. The robot's right arm can rotate with the mouse.

4. The flame shakes up and down according to the rule of trigonometric function. As the mouse is in different areas of the screen, the flame changes to different colors.

5. The mouse is followed by a randomly changing ball, the mouse moves, and the ball moves with the bounce.

6. The background is set to a beat of random size and random color.

7. The belly of the robot has a small star that is constantly changing. As the mouse is in different areas of the screen, the star will also change to a different color.
