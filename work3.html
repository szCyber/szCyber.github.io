Design philosophy


1. The entire interactive work introduces the process of bees collecting honey to make them understand science and nature in a lively and interesting storyline.


2.The interaction mode is novel. The figure dance and drag module make children familiar with the process of honey gathering and more participation.


3. Music and ancient poems make the entire design and art full of charm, increase children's knowledge, and improve aesthetics
